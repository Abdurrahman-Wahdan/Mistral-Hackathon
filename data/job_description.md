# Zephlen: Agentic AI Developer
**Location:** Remote / Hybrid (Istanbul, Turkey)
**Job Type:** Full-time

## About Zephlen
Welcome to Zephlen. Born and proudly based in Istanbul, Turkey, we are a pioneering AI company dedicated to building the next generation of autonomous digital workers. We don't just build chatbots; we build intelligent agents capable of reasoning, planning, and executing complex workflows across the digital ecosystem. Our mission is to augment human productivity by automating the mundane and expanding the boundaries of what software can achieve autonomously, starting from the Turkish tech ecosystem and scaling globally.

If you are obsessed with LLMs, function calling, orchestration layers, and building robust systems around non-deterministic intelligence, Zephlen is the place for you.

## The Role
As an Agentic AI Developer at Zephlen, you will be at the forefront of AI engineering. You will design, build, and scale autonomous agents that interact with APIs, databases, and web environments. You will move beyond simple RAG pipelines and build multi-step reasoning engines that can handle ambiguity, recover from errors, and seamlessly interact with human operators.

This is a hardcore software engineering role at the intersection of backend infrastructure and frontier AI.

## What You Will Do
*   **Design Agent Architectures:** Architect and implement complex agent workflows using state-of-the-art frameworks (or building custom ones) that involve planning, memory management, and tool execution.
*   **Develop Robust Tooling:** Build reliable, secure, and fast internal APIs and tools that our agents will use to interact with the outside world.
*   **Prompt Engineering & Optimization:** Craft, version, and evaluate complex system prompts and few-shot examples. Optimize for token efficiency, latency, and high-fidelity structured outputs (JSON/Pydantic).
*   **Observability & Evaluation:** Build robust evaluation pipelines to rigorously test agent performance, measure hallucination rates, and detect regressions. Implement tracing and observability for multi-step LLM operations.
*   **Scale Production Systems:** Work closely with backend and infrastructure teams to deploy agents in highly concurrent, scalable, and secure environments. Deal with rate limits, context window management, and fallback strategies.

## What We Are Looking For (Requirements)
*   **Experience:** 3+ years of software engineering experience, with at least 1 year deeply focused on building with LLMs in production environments.
*   **Strong Programming Skills:** Expert-level proficiency in **Python** or **TypeScript/Node.js**. 
*   **Agentic Frameworks:** Hands-on experience with frameworks like LangChain, LlamaIndex, AutoGen, CrewAI, Semantic Kernel, or building custom orchestration loops from scratch.
*   **Deep LLM Mechanics:** Strong understanding of how LLMs work under the hood. Expertise in function/tool calling, structured data extraction, RAG techniques (semantic routing, self-querying), and context window optimization.
*   **Backend Engineering:** Solid backend fundamentals. Experience with REST/GraphQL APIs, webhooks, databases (Vector DBs like Pinecone, Weaviate, Qdrant, plus traditional SQL/NoSQL), and asynchronous programming.
*   **Problem Solver:** An "agentic" personality. You are a self-starter who thrives in ambiguity and can independently drive projects from whiteboard to production.

## Preferred Qualifications (Bonus Points)
*   Experience with LLM evaluation frameworks (e.g., RAGAS, DeepEval) and observability tools (LangSmith, Phoenix, Datadog LLM Observability).
*   Familiarity with fine-tuning open-source models (Llama 3, Mistral) for specific tasks or function calling.
*   Contributions to open-source AI/Agent projects or a strong portfolio of personal AI hackathon projects.
*   Experience deploying AI applications using Docker, Kubernetes, and major cloud providers (AWS, GCP).
