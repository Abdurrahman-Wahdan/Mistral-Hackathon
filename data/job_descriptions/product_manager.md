# Job Description: Product Manager

**Department:** Product  
**Level:** Mid/Senior  
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## Role Summary
Own product direction and execution for critical user journeys. You will define priorities, align cross-functional teams, and deliver measurable business outcomes.

## Core Responsibilities
- Define product vision, roadmap, and success metrics.
- Write clear PRDs and prioritize backlog with engineering/design.
- Run discovery interviews and synthesize user feedback.
- Coordinate releases and track adoption/performance.
- Drive alignment across leadership and execution teams.

## Required Skills & Tooling
- **Product:** Roadmapping, prioritization, KPI definition
- **Research:** User interviews, JTBD, usability synthesis
- **Data:** SQL basics, analytics tools (Amplitude/Mixpanel)
- **Delivery:** Jira/Linear, sprint planning, release coordination
- **Communication:** Executive updates, stakeholder management

## What Good Looks Like
- Crisp product thinking with clear tradeoff decisions.
- Ability to turn ambiguity into actionable plans.
- Strong ownership, accountability, and outcome orientation.
