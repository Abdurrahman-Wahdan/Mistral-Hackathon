# Job Description: Data Scientist

**Department:** Data & AI  
**Level:** Mid/Senior  
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## Role Summary
Drive product and business decisions with data. You will build predictive models, run experiments, and partner with engineering to productionize ML solutions.

## Core Responsibilities
- Build and validate forecasting/classification models.
- Design A/B tests and evaluate impact of product changes.
- Translate business questions into analytical frameworks.
- Build dashboards and communicate findings to stakeholders.
- Work with engineers to deploy and monitor models in production.

## Required Tech Stack
- **Languages:** Python, SQL
- **ML Libraries:** scikit-learn, XGBoost, PyTorch
- **Data Tools:** Pandas, Jupyter, dbt
- **Data Platforms:** BigQuery/Snowflake
- **MLOps:** Model tracking, feature pipelines, experiment logging
- **Visualization:** Tableau/Looker or Plotly

## What Good Looks Like
- Strong statistical reasoning and experiment design.
- Ability to explain technical findings in plain language.
- Practical model deployment mindset, not notebook-only work.
