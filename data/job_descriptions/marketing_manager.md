# Job Description: Marketing Manager

**Department:** Growth / Marketing  
**Level:** Mid/Senior  
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## Role Summary
Lead multi-channel marketing strategy to grow pipeline, awareness, and conversion. You will own campaign planning, messaging, and performance optimization.

## Core Responsibilities
- Build quarterly growth plans and campaign calendars.
- Own content, email, paid, and organic acquisition initiatives.
- Define positioning and messaging with Product/Sales.
- Track funnel metrics and optimize CAC-to-conversion performance.
- Manage external partners and internal cross-functional execution.

## Required Skills & Tooling
- **Channels:** SEO, paid media, email lifecycle, social/content
- **Analytics:** GA4, attribution, dashboard reporting
- **Experimentation:** A/B testing, landing page optimization
- **Tooling:** CRM and marketing automation platforms
- **Execution:** Campaign planning, budget allocation, performance tracking

## What Good Looks Like
- Strong growth intuition backed by data discipline.
- Clear written communication and messaging quality.
- Consistent delivery of measurable pipeline impact.
