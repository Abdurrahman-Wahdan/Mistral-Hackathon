## **5. Candidate Improvement Actions**
1. **Develop active listening and empathy**:
   - **Action**: Before rejecting a stakeholder’s request, **ask clarifying questions** (e.g., *"What’s the business impact if we delay this?"*). Practice **paraphrasing their concerns** to show understanding.
   - **Why**: Demonstrates respect and **reduces perceived adversarialness**.

2. **Replace confrontation with structured persuasion**:
   - **Action**: When pushing back, **frame trade-offs with data** (e.g., *"This approach risks X downtime; here’s a 3-step alternative that meets 80% of your needs with 20% of the risk"*).
   - **Why**: Shifts the dynamic from **personal conflict** to **collaborative problem-solving**.

3. **Post-conflict relationship repair**:
   - **Action**: After a disagreement, **proactively check in** with the stakeholder (e.g., *"I want to ensure we’re aligned—how can we adjust to better meet your goals?"*).
   - **Why**: **Rebuilds trust** and signals **accountability** for team dynamics.
